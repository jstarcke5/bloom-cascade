Bloom Cascade — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Bloom Cascade does not collect your personal data. It is a single-player puzzle game with no account, no login, and no analytics or advertising SDKs.

What we store

Your game progress — levels cleared, stars, coins, your garden, and settings — is saved on your device. If you are signed into iCloud, this same progress may sync privately through your own iCloud account (Apple's CloudKit) so it is available on your other devices. We never see this data; it lives in your device and your private iCloud.

What we do not do

  • We do not collect, sell, or share personal information.
  • We do not use third-party advertising or tracking.
  • We do not require an account or your email address.

Purchases

Optional in-app purchases (coins, a booster bundle, and the one-time “Endless Blooms” unlock) are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your payment details. Purchases are optional and are never required to play or finish a level.

Children

Bloom Cascade is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. Because we collect no personal data, we collect none from children.
