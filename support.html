Frequently asked

Do I need to pay to play?

No. Bloom Cascade is free, and coins are never required to finish a level. Hearts refill on their own — one every 20 minutes. If you’d rather never wait, the one-time Endless Blooms unlock removes the wait forever.

I bought Endless Blooms on another device.

Open Shop and tap Restore (top right). Your unlock will be restored.

Does my progress sync between devices?

Yes — if you’re signed into the same iCloud account, your progress and garden sync privately through your own iCloud. There’s no account to create.

Is it playable offline?

Absolutely. Everything works without a network connection.

I’m colorblind — can I tell the flowers apart?

Yes. Every flower has a distinct shape as well as a color, so matches stay clear.
